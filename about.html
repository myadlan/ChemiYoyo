About

We develop this mobile application to increase knowledge of chemistry among SPM students. This is because, nowadays students are more attracted to electronic gadgets. This application can be installed in any electronic gadgets which Certified.

Secondly, this mobile application also developed to produce simple formula that are needed by students to improve their thinking and learning skill for contribute in their studies.

This application is able to produce test in online system which capable to produce questions and marks on-the-spot.

Besides that, we want to encourage students to manage their free time with knowledgeable materials. With this application, parents or students can download this application on their gadgets. So they can study at anytime and anywhere. Parents also do not have to buy books for their children in bookstores or send their children to the library to read books.

Lastly, we want the teenagers feel comfortable and understand more in the easier manner. Therefore, we introduce some animations and sounds that can attract students to study. With this attraction, we hope more students will more familiar with the Chemistry subject.
